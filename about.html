Learn more about Tumba Women Cooperative

Tumba Women Cooperative

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering women through education, skill development, and collaborative initiatives. Our mission is to foster leadership, economic independence, and social well-being for women in Sinyea Town and beyond.

  • Promoting access to education and training
  • Supporting women-led businesses and entrepreneurship
  • Organizing community outreach and advocacy programs
  • Encouraging sustainable development and self-reliance
  • Building a network of support and mentorship
  • Fostering a culture of collaboration and empowerment
  • Creating opportunities for personal and professional growth
